01 What this does for you

The most time-consuming part of every report is transcribing records into the chain of title and the lien and mortgage sections. This tool does that part for you — and keeps you in control of the final sign-off.

⚡

Auto-fills the report

Paste your recorder index results — it sorts every document into chain of title, mortgages, satisfactions, assignments, and the right lien category.

📄

Your exact format

It outputs the Title Search Report template you already use — same sections, same fields — ready to send to the client as a PDF.

✓

You stay in control

Every parsed record is shown for review first. Uncheck anything that belongs to another property, fix anything, then fill. Nothing is committed until you say so.

🔒

Nothing leaves your computer

It runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no cloud, no data sent anywhere. Your search data stays on your machine.

02 Set it up in 3 steps

Download the file

Click Download the tool above. You'll get one file: Apex_Title_Form_Filler.html. Save it somewhere easy — your Desktop is perfect.

Double-click to open

It opens in your default browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari — any of them). That's it. There's nothing to install and no account to create.

Bookmark it (optional)

Drag the open tab to your bookmarks bar so it's one click away every morning. The file works offline forever — keep a copy in your records folder as a backup.

03 How to use it on a file

Import the records

Paste your recorder grantor/grantee search results into the Import box (or upload a CSV/TSV export) and click Parse records. New to it? Click Load demo records first to see it work.

Review & confirm

The tool shows every document and where it's routing each one. Fix any it guessed wrong, uncheck anything for a different property, then click Fill the form.

Verify, then deliver

The report fills on the right. Add the items that need your judgment (tax status, legal description, title opinion), then Print / Save PDF — it names the file to your standard automatically.

🛡️
Built for defensibility. The tool assembles the record; you make the call. It prints a DRAFT watermark and a standard scope disclaimer until you've reviewed it — so an unverified report never goes out the door.
