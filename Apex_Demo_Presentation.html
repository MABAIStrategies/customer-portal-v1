The bottleneck today

Title abstracting is expert work buried in re-typing.

  • The same data, keyed twice. Recorder results get hand-copied into the report, section by section.Slow, and every transcription is a chance for an error to reach the client.
  • Chains assembled by hand. Sorting deeds, mortgages, satisfactions and liens into order eats the morning.
  • Leads go cold. A new order that isn't answered fast often walks to another abstractor within hours.
  • The back half is manual too. Review, deliver, log, follow up — all by hand, on every file.

Built to be safe

Private by default. Compliant by design.

  • Client PII never leaves the machine. No backend, no login, no transmission — nothing to breach.
  • Records access stays within terms. The co-pilot opens public portals and parses your own exports; it does not bypass logins, CAPTCHAs, or subscription walls.
  • Clear scope of work. Output is labeled a title abstract, not a title opinion or insurance — protecting Apex.
  • You review and sign every report before a client ever sees it.
