01 What this does for you

The slow part of every search is pulling and transcribing records into a clean report. Apex pulls the Recorder, Assessment/Tax, Court, and State-Lien data for one address and drafts your North-Star report — with you in control of the final sign-off.

⚡

Assembles the draft

One address pulls Recorder, Tax, Court and State-Lien records and routes each into the chain of title, mortgages, and lien sections automatically.

⚑

Flags what needs you

Open mortgages, possible other-property, chain gaps, and anything unverified are highlighted in red so your judgment goes exactly where it matters.

📄

Your branded output

Download an Apex-branded PDF or generate a Google Doc for sign-off — in the report format you already use.

🔒

Stays on your machine

The app runs locally at localhost. No account, no cloud upload of client files — your search data never leaves your computer.

02 Get started

A one-time setup, then it's one click every morning.

1

Install Node.js (one time)

Download the LTS installer from nodejs.org and run it. This is the engine the app runs on — you only do this once.

2

Double-click the launcher for your computer

Mac: start_apex.command · Windows: start_apex.bat · Linux: start_apex.sh. It starts the app and opens your browser automatically.

3

Your browser opens to the app

It lands on http://localhost:8787. Type a Delaware address, click Generate, and you're working. (Keep the launcher window open while you use it.)
